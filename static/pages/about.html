Remote Slide

Remotely control your slides/presentations from your Smartphone!

About

Remote Slide provides an incredibly simple way to remotely control your presentations, for example on Google Slides, slides.com, Prezi or PowerPoint.

It's really easy to set up. You don't need to install any external applications - but you can, if you want to!
Just drag the bookmark into your bookmarks bar, scan the QR code on your Smartphone and click the bookmark on the page you want to control. That's it!

Alternatively, you can install the browser extension for Google Chrome, to get access to more features.

Features

  • Control your presentation in just 3 steps!
  • No need for external applications
  • Control via buttons, or by swiping on your screen
  • Virtual Laser Pointer to show off the important parts of your presentation!
    • Customizable color & size
  • Live preview of your current slide
  • Timer, to keep track of your presentation pace (Coming Soon)
